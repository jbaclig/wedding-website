February 1, 2019

Jon & Kelly

San Diego, CA

Our Story

 
 
 
 
 
Summer 2009

The Beginning

Kelly, an upcoming Junior at UCSB, and Jon, an upcoming Sophomore, move into separate units at 6628 Pasado Rd., a duplex in Isla Vista, CA with a shared garage. Little did they know the person they'd spend the rest of their lives with was just across that garage.

Xtravaganza - May 2010

Their First Conversation

After almost a whole school year, Kelly & Jon finally have their first conversation when she comes over to ask for some Sprite. Kelly needed it because she was starting to feel sick, but after working all day Jon really needed the Sprite for chaser. Whether it was friendly banter (like Jon remembers) or Jon just being rude (like Kelly remembers), this conversation sparked something in both of them. From this day on they started to talk and hangout together more and more.

 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Summer 2010

The Summer of Snappa
(& LOVE)

With their relationship beginning to grow, Kelly & Jon spend the Summer together playing Snappa (their new favorite beer game) and anxiously awaiting Kanye's weekly G.O.O.D. Friday releases. For the next year they spend as much time with each other as they can. From music festivals to random food trips and everything in between, Kelly & Jon enjoy all kinds of adventures together during the rest of their college years.

June 2011

Kelly Moves to Long Beach

After Kelly graduates from UCSB, Kelly & Jon face their relationship's first test: long distance. Kelly moves to Long Beach while Jon stays behind in Isla Vista to finish his last year at UCSB. Although they can no longer spend as much time with each other as they wanted, the distance was close enough for them to consistently visit each other. But the time apart also made both of them realize just how much they missed each other. As the saying goes, "Absence makes the heart grow fonder."

 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
May 2012

A Special Gift

After months of Kelly asking Jon to get her a dog, Jon comes across an opportunity to do just that. And thus began life with their new dog, Gobi. From then on Gobi was with them everywhere, even when Kelly went to visit Jon back in Isla Vista.

June 2012

Jon Follows Kelly to Long Beach

After Jon graduates from UCSB, he immediately moves out to Long Beach to finally live with Kelly again. Their relationship continues to grow and faces another challenge: moving in together. They had their ups and downs during this time, but everything they went through only made their relationship that much stronger.

 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
January 2012

Another Addition to the Family

Kelly & Jon decide that Gobi needs a new dog sibling in her life to play with while they're away from their appartment. After searching for weeks, they come across a fluffy little dog named Chubby on Craigslist. It was not quite love-at-first-sight for Chubby, but he quickly became a sweet and loving brother for Gobi.

January 2014

An International Adventure

After years of talking about wanting to visit Thailand, Kelly & Jon finally pull the trigger and take a trip there as well as the country of Jon's family, The Philippines. Throughout the month long trip, they enjoyed the sights, food, culture, and the company of each other on an adventure through Southeast Asia. They made lots of new friends as well as bonded with existing ones, but the opportunity for Jon to share his culture with Kelly was the most memorable part about their whole trip.

 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
March 2014

Moving to America's Finest City

After returning from their trip abroad, Kelly and Jon decide that they want to spend their next chapter in the city where some of their closest friends are living in: San Diego. They both find jobs and move there as quickly as they can. They immediately fall in love with everything the city has to offer. The picturesque hikes, beautiful dog beaches for Gobi and Chubby to enjoy, and world-class beer scene keep them busy in San Diego while trips to Portland, Seatlle, Chicago, Austin, and Mexico keep their travel itch satisfied.

October 8, 2017

The Proposal

After seven years of dating, Jon finally makes a plan to propose to Kelly. Kelly was in San Francisco for the weekend, so Jon's plan was to pick her up and surprise her with a proposal on Coronado Island with all her friends and family there to celebrate. As Jon is putting his plan in motion, he gets a call from Kelly saying that her flight's been cancelled. As Jon is panicking trying to figure out what to do now that all her family and friends are in San Diego for the proposal, Kelly gets on a later flight which arrives two hours later than planned. Although Jon knows Kelly likes to go to bed early, he goes through with the plan anyway. He gets down on one knee and pops the question. After a momment of confusion and shock, Kelly says yes! Kelly & Jon are now officialy (and FINALLY) engaged.

 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 

The Present

Today Kelly & Jon are living in San Diego with their two dogs Gobi and Chubby, hanging out at their favorite dog beach, supporting their favorite local breweries, and preparing for their big day. They are so thankful to everyone that had supported them through this time in their life, and they can't wait to celebrate with their closest freinds and family.
