Things to Do

Planning on attending the ceremony and reception and wondering what you should do in between? Don't worry, Jon and Kelly got you covered! Below you'll find a selection of some of their favorite places to hangout near the ceremony and reception venues. The Little Italy neighborhood where the ceremony is located features many of San Diego's best restaurants and bars, while the Point Loma area where the reception is located features a couple of the best breweries San Diego has to offer and other exciting food and drink options. There are many more places than the ones listed below, and Kelly and Jon hope you get a change to explore two of their favorite neighborhoods in the city!

Ballast Point Little Italy

2215 India St, San Diego, CA 92101

Ballast Point's Little Italy location is located about half a mile away from Our Lady of The Rosary Church. Although smaller than their more recent locations, the Little Italy location boasts a large outdoor patio, a delicious food menu, and 40+ taps of Ballast Point beers ranging from the classics to R&D special releases.

Bottlecraft Little Italy

2252 India St, San Diego, CA 92101

Located right across the street from Ballast Point, Bottlecraft is a tasting room and bottleshop featuring a selection of great local, national, and international craft beers. Stop in for a couple tasters or to browse their selection of bottles and cans to take home. This is a must stop for any craft beer lover.

Vino Carta

2161 India St, San Diego, CA 92101

Vino Carta is a neighborhood wine bar and bottleshop specializing in small, family run producers and (Jon's newest obsession) natural wines. If you're a wine lover looking for something a little bit different, this is definitely a great place to spend an hour or two.

Barbusa

1917 India St, San Diego, CA 92101

Barbusa is a modern Italian restaurant featuring one of Jon & Kelly's favorite happy hours (M-F 3-6PM). If you're hungry, stop in to indulge in the great happy hour food menu featuring pasta bolognese, sausage polenta, stuffed squash blossoms, and many more delicious Italian dishes.

Kettner Exchange

2001 Kettner Blvd, San Diego, CA 92101

Kettner Exchange is the perfect spot to grab a cocktail or two while enjoying the beatufiul San Diego sun. Featuring a great rooftop bar and patio as well as an Asian-inspired food menu, this is a nice spot to unwind for an hour or two.

Modern Times Beer

3725 Greenwood St, San Diego, CA 92110

One of San Diego's best breweries is only a couple miles away from BRICK and Humphreys Half Moon Inn. From Hazy IPAs, funky saisons, full-bodied stouts, and barrel aged sours, Modern Times has something for everyone. You'll also find an awesome coffee shop featuring house roasted beans, sweet merch, and specialty release bottles and cans available to bring home.

Liberty Public Market

2820 Historic Decatur Rd, San Diego, CA 92106

San Diego's equivalent of Anaheim's Packing House or LA's Grand Central Market, the Liberty Public Market is a great place to check out local San Diego creators. Located right next to BRICK, you can grab a bite to eat at one of the many food stalls, a craft brew from Bottlecraft, or any other drink you like at the full bar all located inside the market.

Stone Brewing World Bistro & Gardens - Liberty Station

2816 Historic Decatur Rd, San Diego, CA 92106

Also located right next to BRICK, Stone's second flagship location boasts a beautiful outdoor garden and a beer list featuring Stone clasics, specialty releases, and guest taps and bottles. Stop by to experience one of the biggest and most successful breweries to call San Diego home.
